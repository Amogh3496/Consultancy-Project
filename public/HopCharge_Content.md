### 1. Company Overview
* **About HopCharge:** HopCharge is India's first on-demand, doorstep EV charging service. It operates a "virtual energy cloud" using specialized vans equipped with proprietary lithium-ion energy pods, eliminating the need for expensive grid upgrades or stationary chargers.
* **Revenue Model:**
  * **Pay-Per-Use:** Ad-hoc charging for emergencies or top-ups.
  * **Subscription (HopchargeX):** Monthly plans for regular, scheduled charging.
  * **B2B / OEM Partnerships:** Bundled charging services for new EV buyers (e.g., Tata.ev).
* **Vision:** To eliminate "charging anxiety" for urban consumers by offering unparalleled, plug-and-play convenience anywhere, anytime.

### 2. Market Analysis
* **TAM (Total Addressable Market):** ~$5.5 Billion (The projected EV charging infrastructure market in India by 2027).
* **SAM (Serviceable Available Market):** ~$1.2 Billion (Urban residential and commercial EV charging demand in Tier-1 Indian cities, specifically areas with high-rise apartments lacking grid capacity).
* **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market):** ~$150 Million (Targeted revenue for premium, on-demand mobile charging solutions in top metropolitan areas over the next 3 years).

### 3. User Persona
* **Persona 1: The High-Rise Resident (Aarav, 34)**
  * **Characteristics:** Lives in a multi-story apartment without a dedicated EV parking charger.
  * **Pain Points:** Wasting time waiting in queues at public fast chargers; extreme "range anxiety" before weekend trips.
  * **Needs:** Reliable, recurring charging at home while he sleeps or relaxes.
* **Persona 2: The Busy Professional (Neha, 29)**
  * **Characteristics:** Drives her EV to the office daily and values her time heavily.
  * **Pain Points:** Wasting 45+ minutes sitting at a public charging station during work hours.
  * **Needs:** Seamless, invisible charging while her car is parked at the office.

### 4. Features (Focus: Improving Customer Retention & ARPU)
1. **Neighborhood Pod-Sharing 🏘️:** Users can "host" a charging slot. If multiple EVs in the same apartment complex or office park book the same HopCharge van in a 2-hour window, all users receive a 15% discount. *(Benefit: Massively improves van utilization and creates community virality).*
2. **Predictive Smart Scheduling 🤖:** The app learns the user's driving patterns and preemptively sends a push notification to schedule a HopCharge van right before the battery drops below 20%. *(Benefit: Reduces cognitive load and increases booking frequency).*
3. **HopCoins Gamification 🪙:** A loyalty system where users earn "HopCoins" for maintaining a charging streak (e.g., booking 3 times a month). Coins can be redeemed for free premium time-slots or priority routing. *(Benefit: Builds habits and increases switching costs).*

### 6. GTM Strategy
* **Phase 1 (Months 1-2): Launch Neighborhood Sharing**
  * Run a pilot program in dense tech parks and specific high-rise societies in Gurgaon/Bengaluru.
  * Distribute referral codes directly to Residents' Welfare Associations (RWAs) to encourage bulk bookings.
* **Phase 2 (Months 3-4): HopCoins Rollout**
  * Introduce the wallet/coin system via a major app update.
  * Seed existing HopchargeX subscribers with bonus coins to kickstart engagement and encourage them to book their next session.
* **Phase 3 (Months 5-6): Predictive Analytics**
  * Soft-launch the predictive scheduling feature to the top 10% of heavy users first to train the algorithm before a wider release.

### 7. Metrics
* **North Star Metric:** 
  * **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** Tracking the financial shift from single ad-hoc users to recurring, monthly subscribers.
* **Primary Metrics:**
  * **Customer Retention Rate:** Percentage of users who successfully book >2 times a month.
  * **Van Utilization Rate:** Average hours per day a HopCharge van is actively dispensing energy vs. traveling in traffic.
* **Secondary Metrics:**
  * **Community Adoption:** Number of multi-car bookings per location (measures the success of Neighborhood Sharing).
  * **Gamification Engagement:** Percentage of HopCoins redeemed within 30 days of earning them.
